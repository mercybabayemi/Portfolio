About Me

Hello! I’m a Nigerian with a deep passion for technology and its potential to advance the human race. My interests lie at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Computational Linguistics where language, data, and intelligent systems meet to solve meaningful problems. With a Bachelor’s degree in English, I bring linguistic and analytical insight into the world of data-driven innovation. Having trained in Software Engineering through a bootcamp, I’m currently expanding my expertise in Machine Learning algorithms, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Data Science, exploring how machines learn, reason, and understand human language. Beyond technology, I’m passionate about giving back through education-focused volunteering, aligning with SDG4 (Quality Education) because I believe that access to learning remains one of the most transformative forces for individuals and communities.
